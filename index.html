About Dr. Masub

The physician behind
your transformation

Dr. Natasha Masub, M.D., is a Board-Certified Dermatologist and Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology, known for her warm, approachable manner and meticulous approach to aesthetic medicine.

After graduating Summa Cum Laude from Brooklyn College's combined BA-MD program, Dr. Masub completed her medical degree at SUNY Downstate Health Sciences University — pursuing a research fellowship in skin fibrosis, earning special distinction in dermatology research, and becoming a member of the Alpha Omega Alpha honor society. She then completed internship at Northwell Plainview Hospital, followed by 3 years of dermatology residency at SUNY Downstate.

Dr. Masub treats acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer alongside a full suite of cosmetic services — neurotoxin injections, dermal fillers, laser treatments, radiofrequency microneedling, and customized skincare plans. She believes the best outcomes come from strong partnerships built on trust and open communication.
